Far far away, behind the word mountains

Far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts. Separated they live in Bookmarksgrove right at the coast of the Semantics, a large language ocean. A small river named Duden flows by their place and supplies it with the necessary regelialia. It is a paradisematic country, in which roasted parts of sentences fly into your mouth.

Top ds3 osts

Main Theme. Starting with the theme played in the main menu, we have this epic song. From now on its just a bunch of placeholders so you can skip to the next one if you like.

Vordt of the Boreal Valley. This one starts calm and smooth, just as the boss's moveset, but from the half on it kicks in with epic vocals and almost makes you need to change your pants. This one actually had more text.

Epilogue. Last but certainly not least we have the last song played in the game, only the ones with guts to beat the game(or to search for it on the internet) had the pleasure of listening to this epic soundtrack.
